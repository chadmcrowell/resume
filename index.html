Azure Training Architect II

I build courses to help people grasp technology in a fun, collaborative and engaging way! You can find my course catalog on acloud.guru. My courses are on subjects such as Azure(Cloud), DevOps, Containers, Kubernetes, PowerShell, Apache Kafka, and more!

I try to take my experience in the field to add value and depth to the learning experience.
